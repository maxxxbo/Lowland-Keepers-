How to Play

  • Build Your Team: Choose your team members wisely
  • Drag & Drop Mechanics: To initiate an attack or trigger an interaction, simply drag your Keeper’s card onto the target—whether it’s an enemy, a spell, or an Artifact.
  • Generate Mana: Defeating enemies grants you mana crystals. Plan your attacks so that multiple foes fall in a single turn, filling your mana pool rapidly.
  • Never End a Turn Unnecessarily: Since enemies grant mana upon death, aim to clear as many threats as possible before passing. The more you clear, the more mana you accumulate—fueling your next round of powerful plays.
